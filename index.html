About Kathleen Capelle

Kathleen earned her MSW from the University of Maryland at Baltimore School of Social Work in 2005, with specialization in Clinical Social Work and Employee Assistance Program. She worked at The Johns Hopkins Hospital prior to moving to Florida in 2007.

In Florida she has worked for hospice and home health agencies, and has six years of experience as a dialysis social worker. She became certified in Symptom Targeted Interventions while working with dialysis patients.

Kathleen is ready to help you find peace of mind today.
