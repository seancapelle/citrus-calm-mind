About

About Kathleen Capelle

Kathleen Capelle is certified as a Practioner for Rapid Resolution Therapy®. She earned her MSW from the University of Maryland at Baltimore School of Social Work in 2005, with specialization in Clinical Social Work and Employee Assistance Program. She worked at The Johns Hopkins Hospital prior to moving to Florida in 2007.
In Florida she has worked for hospice and home health agencies, and has six years of experience as a dialysis social worker. She became certified in Symptom Targeted Interventions while working with dialysis patients.
Kathleen is ready to help you find peace of mind today.

About Jon Connelly

Jon Connelly is the creator of Rapid Resolution Therapy®. Thousands of mental health professionals participate in his advanced clinical trainings.
He is the author of "Life Changing Conversations - The Power of Transformational Communication" which demonstrates that personal transformation can be facilitated in a single meeting.
He is also the founder of The Institute for Survivors of Sexual Violence, a non-profit corporation.
